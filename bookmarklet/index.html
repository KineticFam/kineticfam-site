Add to Kinetic — one click from any page

See a school events page? Doctor portal reminder? Sports schedule? Send it to your family's briefing with a single click. No app, no copy-paste.

What's a bookmarklet? It's a special bookmark that runs a tiny script instead of opening a page — drag it to your bookmarks bar once, then click it on any website to instantly forward that page to Kinetic.

How it works

🔖
Step 1
Drag to your bookmarks bar
One-time setup. Drag the "Add to Kinetic" button above to your browser's bookmarks bar. Takes 3 seconds.
🌐
Step 2
Browse to any page
Visit a school events page, doctor portal, sports schedule, pharmacy reminder — any page with information you want to track.
📤
Step 3
Click "Add to Kinetic"
A small overlay appears. Click send — the page title and URL go straight to your Kinetic address. Kinetic's AI picks out the dates and adds them to your briefing.

We never see your browsing — you choose what to send

The bookmarklet runs entirely in your browser. We don't track what pages you visit, what you click, or where you go online. When you click "Add to Kinetic," only the page title and URL you explicitly choose to share are sent — nothing else. You're always in control.
