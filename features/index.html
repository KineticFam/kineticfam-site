Everything Your Family Needs. One Email. Every Morning.

Here's what Kinetic puts in your family's 6:30 AM morning briefing — and how each piece gets there automatically.

📧 Smart Email Forwarding

Your Kinetic address is the hub for everything. Forward any email — school newsletter, sports schedule, dentist confirmation, birthday invite — and Kinetic reads it, extracts the relevant dates and details, and adds them to your family's picture automatically. No copy-pasting. No manual calendar entries.

  • Forward school newsletters → Kinetic extracts every date and event
  • Forward appointment confirmations → reminder appears in your briefing
  • Forward sports schedules → upcoming games appear before each one
  • Forward birthday party invites → date saved, surfaces when it's approaching

📸 Photo & PDF Extraction

Got a paper schedule? A PDF the coach sent? A photo of the fridge calendar? Attach it to an email to your Kinetic address. Kinetic's AI reads images and documents and pulls out every date and event — no manual transcription required.

📅 Today's Family Schedule

Every morning, Kinetic compiles every event for every family member happening that day into one clean view. No switching between calendars. No asking "wait, what time is that thing?" The answer is in the briefing, before anyone asks.

📆 Two-Week Lookahead

Upcoming events aren't just shown on the day they happen — they surface in your briefing as they approach, with enough lead time to actually prepare. Birthday gift needed Friday? You'll see it Monday. Permission slip due next week? It's in Tuesday's briefing.

🌤️ Local Weather

Current conditions, today's high and low, rain probability, and UV index — for your specific zip code. Automatically included every morning so you know what to pack before you leave the house.

📰 Daily Headlines

Five top national stories and local news from your area. Enough to stay informed without spending 20 minutes on your phone before the kids wake up.

🎉 Events Near You

Kinetic automatically surfaces family-friendly events within 30 miles of your home — festivals, concerts, museum events, sports games, community activities. Discover what's happening this weekend without having to go looking.

🔔 Custom Reminders

Put "Reminder" in the subject line of any email to your Kinetic address and it appears in your morning briefing for seven days. Perfect for things that don't quite fit on a calendar: "Pick up dry cleaning," "Call the pediatrician," "Return library books."

🎂 Annual Birthdays

Forward a birthday email or put "Birthday" in the subject line with a name and date. Kinetic remembers it every year and surfaces it a week before so you actually have time to do something about it.

🏈 Sports Schedules

Forward ticket confirmations, team schedule emails, or any sports-related message — Kinetic adds upcoming games to your morning briefing. Works for every major league: NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, college football, college basketball, WNBA, MLS, and more.

🏫 School Calendar Integration

Forward the district calendar PDF at the start of the school year. Kinetic reads every date — holidays, early releases, teacher workdays, breaks — and surfaces each one in your morning briefing as it approaches. Works with any school in any district.

🔒 Privacy First

Kinetic never connects to your personal email, calendar, or any other account. It only knows what you send it. Your family's information is used exclusively to generate your morning briefing — never sold, shared, or used to train AI models.
