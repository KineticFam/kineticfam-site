2025-2026 Key School Calendar Dates

The official Fairfax County Public Schools calendar is published each spring for the following school year. Key dates typically include:

  • First day of school — late August or early September
  • Labor Day — first Monday in September (no school)
  • Fall break / Columbus Day — typically mid-October
  • Thanksgiving break — Wednesday before through the weekend
  • Winter break — late December through early January
  • Martin Luther King Jr. Day — third Monday in January
  • Presidents' Day / Winter break — mid-February
  • Spring break — typically mid-March or early April
  • Memorial Day — last Monday in May (no school)
  • Last day of school — typically late May or mid-June

For the official 2025-2026 calendar with exact dates, visit the Fairfax County Public Schools district website directly.
