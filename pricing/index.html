Questions & answers

Everything you need to know before signing up.

Every morning at 6:30 AM, Kinetic sends a personalized email that includes: your family's events for the day, tomorrow's weather, local news headlines, and — on Family/Pro plans — sports scores and school calendar reminders. It pulls from emails you've forwarded, PDFs you've snapped, and calendars you've connected. No app to open.
Forward any email to your family's unique Kinetic address (like darlington@kineticfam.com) and our AI pulls out the dates, times, and details. You can also forward PDFs and photos of paper schedules — we handle those too. On Family+ plans, you can connect iCal feeds from school websites so reminders come in automatically.
We take privacy seriously. Emails you forward are processed to extract calendar events, then discarded — we don't store the original email content. Your family data is never sold, never used for advertising, and never shared with third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account settings. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
