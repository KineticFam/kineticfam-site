Common questions.

Is it really free?

Yes. The free plan includes the full daily briefing forever. No credit card required. You'll get every morning's briefing — weather, schedule, news — without paying a thing.

What's included in the free plan?

Everything: school calendars, weather, sports, news, local events, smart email forwarding, the full briefing. The free plan is genuinely complete — not a stripped-down trial.

What do I get with the paid plan?

SMS morning text summaries, more family member slots (up to 6 recipients), iCal sync, school calendar auto-loading, and priority support. Family Pro adds Google Calendar two-way sync and LMS integrations.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, immediately, no questions asked. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your account settings at any time. If you cancel, you keep access through the end of your billing period, then drop to Free.

Is my family's data private?

Absolutely. We never sell or share your data. Emails you forward are processed to extract calendar events, then deleted. Your family's schedule is yours.
