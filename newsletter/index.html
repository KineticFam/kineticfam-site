How Your Schedule Gets Into It

The briefing builds itself from what you send it. Forward a school newsletter email — Kinetic reads it and pulls out every date. Attach a PDF sports schedule — Kinetic extracts every game. Snap a photo of the schedule on the fridge — Kinetic reads that too. Or just type it: "Emma's recital, Saturday 2pm."

Whatever you send in, Kinetic adds to your family's picture and surfaces it in your morning briefing when it's relevant. No manual calendar entry. No app to open. The newsletter arrives at 6:30 AM whether or not you thought to check anything.

Why Email (Not an App)

Every adult in your family already checks email every morning. Kinetic works with that habit instead of requiring you to build a new one. There's no app to download, no notification to enable, no password to share with your partner. The briefing goes to your normal email address, in a format anyone can read.

This also means the whole family benefits without the whole family needing to sign up. One person subscribes. Everyone gets the briefing.

How It Differs from a Family Newsletter Service

Traditional family newsletter tools (Tinybeans, family group chats, shared Google Docs) require someone to curate and write the content. Kinetic is different: it synthesizes information you've already sent it and generates a briefing automatically. The intelligence is in the synthesis — understanding what matters today, what's coming up, what's worth surfacing.

Think of it less like a newsletter service and more like a very organized household assistant who reads your emails, watches your calendar, and sends you a daily briefing before the day starts.
