Kinetic Family SMS Program

An optional daily text message briefing for families on the go. Here's exactly how it works.

Program name
Kinetic Family Daily Briefing SMS
Message frequency
Up to 1 message per day. Messages only sent when you have events scheduled, or daily if the full morning briefing is enabled.
Message type
Informational — your family's daily schedule, event times, and locations. No marketing or promotional messages.
How to opt in
Check the optional SMS box at signup (above on this page) or in your account settings. Phone number verification required before messages begin.
How to opt out
Reply STOP to any message at any time. Immediate unsubscription. One confirmation sent. No further messages.
Help
Reply HELP to any message, email admin@kineticfam.com, or visit kineticfam.com/support.
Rates
Msg & data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan. Kinetic does not charge separately for SMS.
Carrier support
Compatible with all major US carriers: AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and regional carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
Full consent language shown at opt-in:

"Yes, I want optional daily SMS briefings from Kinetic Family (Kinetic Harmony LLC). Up to 1 msg/day. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel. Reply HELP for help. See Privacy Policy and Terms."

Consent is: Optional (unchecked by default) · Not required to use the service · Not shared with or transferred to any third party · Logged with timestamp and consent version for your records.

Your mobile number is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party for any purpose. See our full Privacy Policy for complete details.
