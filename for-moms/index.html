The invisible workload no one talks about

🧠

The mental tabs you keep open

Soccer cleats need to be cleaned. Emma's dentist appointment is Thursday. Sign-ups for the spring play close Friday. It's exhausting keeping all of it loaded in your head.

📩

The email avalanche

School newsletters, teacher updates, class party sign-ups, PTA announcements — every one of them contains something you might need to act on. You can't ignore them and you can't read them all.

📅

"Wait, when is that again?"

You've told them three times. It's in the calendar. You texted it. And somehow you're still the person who has to remember and remind everyone, every single morning.

🔔

The anticipatory planning

You're not just remembering events. You're thinking ahead — who needs a ride, what needs to be packed, whether the snacks for the game were bought. It's a full-time job on top of your full-time job.

Research consistently shows that this "cognitive labor" — the anticipating, planning, and tracking of family logistics — falls disproportionately on mothers. You know it. We built Kinetic because it doesn't have to.

How Kinetic takes it off your plate

Kinetic isn't an app you have to remember to open. It's an AI briefing that arrives every morning at 6:30 AM — in your email, and everyone else's. No setup required after the first 2 minutes.

1

Forward once, remember forever

See a school newsletter with important dates? Forward it to your Kinetic address. Kinetic's AI reads it, extracts every date and deadline, and adds them to your family's briefing. PDFs, photos of paper schedules, even sports team emails — all handled.

2

Everyone gets the same briefing

On Family plans, you add your partner, your older kids — anyone who needs to know. Every morning, they each get the same briefing. You stop being the only one who knows what's happening. The mental load gets distributed.

3

School calendars, automatically loaded

Enter your school district and Kinetic loads the holiday schedule, early release days, and break weeks automatically. No more discovering on Sunday night that school is closed Monday.

4

No app to check. It just shows up.

There's no app to remember to open, no screen to check before bed. Kinetic emails your family every morning. It meets them where they already are — in their inbox.
