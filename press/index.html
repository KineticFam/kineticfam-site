The One-Paragraph Summary

Kinetic is an AI-powered daily morning briefing for families. Every morning at 6:30 AM, Kinetic sends a personalized email with the family's schedule, a two-week lookahead, local weather, top news headlines, nearby events, birthdays, and reminders. Families add events by forwarding emails, attaching PDFs, snapping photos of paper schedules, or typing directly. No app download required. Kinetic is built on a privacy-first model — it never connects to personal email or calendar accounts. Kinetic is a product of Kinetic Harmony LLC, based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

What Makes Kinetic Different

Most family calendar apps are containers — they wait for you to open them and enter information. Kinetic works differently in two key ways:

  • AI-powered input. Families forward emails, attach PDFs, or snap photos. Kinetic reads and extracts the relevant dates automatically. No manual entry.
  • Proactive delivery. Instead of a calendar you have to check, Kinetic delivers a synthesized daily briefing every morning before the day starts. One email. Everything you need to know.

The result is a product that reduces the "mental load" of family scheduling — the invisible cognitive labor of tracking, remembering, and synthesizing a household's worth of information.
