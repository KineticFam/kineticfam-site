Your Family's Privacy

At Kinetic, your family's trust is everything. Here's how we protect your information — and why you can feel confident using Kinetic every day.

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

When you sign up, we ask for just the basics:

  • Your last name — so we can personalize your newsletter
  • Your zip code — so we can deliver local weather and nearby events
  • Email addresses of family members who should receive the newsletter

That's it. No passwords. No payment info. No social media accounts.

Your Family Calendar

Your family gets a private, dedicated calendar that only your authorized family members can add to. Here's how it works:

  • Only emails from addresses you've approved can add events to your calendar
  • No one outside your family can see or modify your schedule
  • Each family's calendar is completely separate — your information is never shared with other Kinetic families

How You Opt In

Kinetic is an opt-in only service. Here's exactly how it works:

  • A parent or guardian visits kineticfam.com and completes the sign-up form
  • The form asks for your family name, zip code, and the email addresses you want to receive the newsletter
  • By submitting the form, you explicitly consent to receive the Kinetic Daily newsletter at the email addresses you provide
  • A confirmation email is sent immediately after sign-up — no newsletters are sent until sign-up is complete
  • You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email, or by emailing admin@kineticfam.com
  • We do not add anyone to the list without their explicit sign-up — no purchased lists, no referrals, no pre-checked boxes

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for one purpose: delivering your personalized Kinetic daily briefing each morning. That means:

  • Pulling weather for your zip code
  • Showing your family's schedule
  • Finding events near you
  • Delivering to the email addresses you've chosen

We never sell, share, or rent your information to anyone. Period.

SMS Communications

Kinetic Family offers an optional SMS service that delivers your family's daily schedule briefing by text message. This section explains how we handle your phone number and SMS communications.

What we collect. When you opt in to SMS updates, we collect your mobile phone number. We also record the date and time you gave consent, and the version of the consent language you agreed to. We do not collect carrier information or device identifiers.

How it is used. Your phone number is used solely to send you schedule-related SMS messages, including:

  • Daily morning briefings with your family's events, times, and locations
  • Event updates or changes you've added to your schedule
  • Occasional reminders tied to events on your calendar

We do not use your phone number for any other purpose, and we do not send promotional, advertising, or third-party messages.

Consent is voluntary. SMS is an optional service — you can use Kinetic Family fully without enrolling in text messages. SMS messages are never sent without your explicit opt-in. Consent is obtained via an unchecked, optional checkbox during signup; checking it is not required to create an account or access any feature. No messages are sent until you complete phone number verification with a one-time code. Consent is logged with a timestamp and retained as part of your account record.

SMS consent is not shared with third parties. Your consent to receive SMS messages from Kinetic Family is not transferred, sold, or shared with any other company or program. Consent applies solely to messages sent by Kinetic Family (operated by Kinetic Harmony LLC).

How to opt out. You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any Kinetic text message — you will be unsubscribed immediately and receive a confirmation
  • Turning off SMS in your Kinetic dashboard (Connect → Morning Texts)
  • Emailing us at admin@kineticfam.com and we will remove your number within 24 hours

Once you opt out, no further messages will be sent. You can opt back in at any time by re-entering your number in your dashboard.

Your data is not sold or shared. We never sell, rent, share, or disclose your phone number to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes. Your number is never used to build audience profiles or shared with data brokers.

Carriers and rates. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier plan. Kinetic is not responsible for charges from your mobile carrier. For support, reply HELP to any message or contact us at admin@kineticfam.com.

Carrier liability. Kinetic is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message delivery depends on your carrier and network conditions.

For more information about how our SMS program works, including opt-in instructions and message examples, visit app.kineticfam.com/text-updates.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

Kinetic is a parent-controlled service. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All accounts are created and managed by parents or legal guardians, who control everything about how their family's information is used.

Because Kinetic is built for families, some information you add to your account naturally relates to your children — their names, their school, their sports schedules, and their upcoming events. Here's exactly how we handle that:

  • All children's data flows through you. A parent or guardian creates the account and adds any information about children. Children never sign up directly, and we never collect information from a child without a parent's knowledge and consent.
  • We collect only what's necessary. We store children's names and schedule information only to the extent needed to personalize and deliver your family's morning briefing. Nothing more.
  • No behavioral advertising — ever. We do not use any information about your children (or anyone in your family) for advertising purposes, and we never will.
  • No sharing with third parties. Information about your children is never sold, rented, or disclosed to any third party for any commercial purpose.
  • Emails are never permanently stored. When you forward a school PDF or send us schedule information, the original email is automatically deleted after we extract the relevant dates and events. We keep the extracted events — not your emails.
  • You can review or delete your children's data at any time. Log in to your dashboard at app.kineticfam.com to view, edit, or remove any event or family member. To delete your account and all associated data entirely, visit Account → Delete Account, or email us at admin@kineticfam.com.
  • We will respond to parental data requests within 30 days. If you want to review what information we hold about your child, request corrections, or ask us to stop collecting it, contact us at admin@kineticfam.com.

If we ever discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information directly from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately. Please contact us at admin@kineticfam.com if you have any concerns.

How We Protect Your Data

  • All data is stored securely and access is strictly limited to the Kinetic system — no third parties
  • All connections to Kinetic are encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
  • We regularly review our security practices to make sure your information stays safe
  • Authorized sender verification means strangers can't add events to your calendar, even if they guess your Kinetic email address
  • Emails you send to your Kinetic address are automatically deleted after processing — we extract the dates and events, then the original email is permanently removed. Your personal emails are never stored.

You're Always in Control

  • View your data anytime at app.kineticfam.com
  • Edit or delete any event directly from your dashboard
  • Add or remove family members anytime from your account settings
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing admin@kineticfam.com — we'll send it within 30 days
  • Delete your account at any time from Account Settings — we'll permanently remove your family's data. No waiting period, no questions asked.
  • Unsubscribe from any email using the link in the footer, or by emailing us directly

Data Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently remove all associated data — including your family's name, email addresses, schedule events, and any other information we hold — within 30 days of your request.

Inbound emails sent to your Kinetic address are deleted immediately after processing. We never archive or retain the original content of emails you send us.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy — particularly anything affecting how children's information is handled — we'll notify you by email before those changes take effect. The date at the top of this page always reflects when it was last updated.
