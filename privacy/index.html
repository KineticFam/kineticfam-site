Your Family's Privacy

At Kinetic, your family's trust is everything. Here's how we protect your information — and why you can feel confident using Kinetic every day.

What We Collect

When you sign up, we ask for just the basics:

  • Your last name — so we can personalize your newsletter
  • Your zip code — so we can deliver local weather and nearby events
  • Email addresses of family members who should receive the newsletter

That's it. No passwords. No payment info. No social media accounts.

Your Family Calendar

Your family gets a private, dedicated calendar that only your authorized family members can add to. Here's how it works:

  • Only emails from addresses you've approved can add events to your calendar
  • No one outside your family can see or modify your schedule
  • Each family's calendar is completely separate — your information is never shared with other Kinetic families

How You Opt In

Kinetic is an opt-in only service. Here's exactly how it works:

  • A parent or guardian visits kineticfam.com and completes the sign-up form
  • The form asks for your family name, zip code, and the email addresses you want to receive the newsletter
  • By submitting the form, you explicitly consent to receive the Kinetic Daily newsletter at the email addresses you provide
  • A confirmation email is sent immediately after sign-up — no newsletters are sent until sign-up is complete
  • You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email, or by emailing admin@kineticfam.com
  • We do not add anyone to the list without their explicit sign-up — no purchased lists, no referrals, no pre-checked boxes

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for one purpose: delivering your personalized Kinetic daily briefing each morning. That means:

  • Pulling weather for your zip code
  • Showing your family's schedule
  • Finding events near you
  • Delivering to the email addresses you've chosen

We never sell, share, or rent your information to anyone. Period.

How We Protect Your Data

  • All calendar and email data is stored securely within Google Workspace, protected by Google's enterprise-grade security
  • Access to family data is strictly limited to the Kinetic system — no third parties
  • We regularly review our security practices to make sure your information stays safe
  • Authorized sender verification means strangers can't add events to your calendar, even if they guess your Kinetic email address
  • Emails you send to your Kinetic address are automatically deleted after processing — we extract the dates and events, then the original email is permanently removed. Your personal emails are never stored.

You're Always in Control

  • Add family members anytime by letting us know
  • Remove family members — just send us a quick email
  • Cancel anytime — we'll delete your calendar and all associated data. No questions asked.

Questions? We're real people.
Just reply to any Kinetic email or reach out to admin@kineticfam.com
