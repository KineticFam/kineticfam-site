Your Family's Privacy

At Kinetic, your family's trust is everything. Here's how we protect your information — and why you can feel confident using Kinetic every day.

Last updated: May 8, 2026

What We Collect

Here is a complete list of the information Kinetic collects and why:

Account & identity information

  • Your family last name — used to personalize your briefing
  • Your zip code — used for local weather and nearby events
  • Email addresses of family members who receive the briefing
  • Partner or co-parent email (optional) — to share access to the family schedule

Family & schedule information

  • Kids' names and ages (provided by you) — used to personalize briefing content; see Children's Privacy section below
  • Calendar events you add — via email, SMS, in-app, or file upload; stored in your family's schedule
  • School name and district — used to sync school calendar events into your briefing
  • iCal feed URLs from school, sports, or other calendar sources you connect
  • Caregiver contacts (names, emails, phone numbers, relationship type) — used to generate Caregiver Briefings when you request them
  • Carpool network contacts — used for carpool coordination features

Connected services

  • Google Calendar OAuth tokens — if you choose to connect Google Calendar, we store an access token and refresh token to sync your calendar. We request read access (to import your events) and, optionally, write access (to push Kinetic-created events back to Google Calendar if you enable Primary Calendar Mode). You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from your Connect tab.
  • Phone number — if you opt in to SMS/RCS alerts; see SMS section below

Technical & usage data

  • AI chat history — messages you send to the Kinetic assistant are stored to provide conversational context and improve responses within your session. See AI Processing below.
  • Push notification device tokens — if you enable push notifications on iOS or web
  • Usage patterns — feature usage, session timing, and event add frequency; used to improve the product and personalize your experience
  • Inbound email content — emails forwarded to your Kinetic address are processed to extract calendar events, then permanently deleted. We do not archive your emails.
  • Photos or files you upload — processed by AI to extract event information, then deleted after parsing

Billing

  • Subscription status, plan type, and trial dates — so we know whether your account is active. Payment card details are never stored by Kinetic; they are held by Stripe or Apple. See Billing & Payment below.

We collect only what's needed to run Kinetic. We don't sell it, we don't share it, and we don't use it for advertising.

Your Family Calendar

Your family gets a private, dedicated calendar that only your authorized family members can add to. Here's how it works:

  • Only emails from addresses you've approved can add events to your calendar
  • No one outside your family can see or modify your schedule
  • Each family's calendar is completely separate — your information is never shared with other Kinetic families

How You Opt In

Kinetic is an opt-in only service. Here's exactly how it works:

  • A parent or guardian visits kineticfam.com and completes the sign-up form
  • The form asks for your family name, zip code, and the email addresses you want to receive the newsletter
  • By submitting the form, you explicitly consent to receive the Kinetic Daily newsletter at the email addresses you provide
  • A confirmation email is sent immediately after sign-up — no newsletters are sent until sign-up is complete
  • You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email, or by emailing admin@kineticfam.com
  • We do not add anyone to the list without their explicit sign-up — no purchased lists, no referrals, no pre-checked boxes

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for one purpose: delivering your personalized Kinetic daily briefing each morning. That means:

  • Pulling weather for your zip code
  • Showing your family's schedule
  • Finding events near you
  • Delivering to the email addresses you've chosen

We never sell, share, or rent your information to anyone. Period.

AI Processing & Your Content

Kinetic uses artificial intelligence to power several features — parsing events from emails and SMS messages, answering questions in the chat assistant, enriching event details, and generating your daily briefing. Here's what that means for your data:

  • AI provider: Kinetic uses the Anthropic API (Claude AI) to process natural language requests, extract calendar events from forwarded messages, and generate briefing content. Content you submit — including forwarded emails, SMS messages, chat messages, and uploaded files — is sent to Anthropic's API for processing.
  • Anthropic's data use: Anthropic does not use API-submitted content to train its models by default. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • What we send to AI: The content of messages you send to Kinetic (email, SMS, chat), along with relevant context about your family's schedule, may be included in API requests. We do not send payment information, passwords, or device identifiers to AI systems.
  • AI-generated content: Your daily briefing, event confirmations, and assistant replies are generated by AI. We review outputs for quality but the AI may occasionally produce inaccurate information — particularly for third-party data like sports scores or school schedules.
  • No AI decisions about your account: AI is not used to make automated decisions that have legal or significant effects on your account (such as billing, suspension, or data deletion). Those decisions are made by humans.

Third-Party Service Providers

Kinetic uses trusted third-party services to operate. Each provider has access only to the information needed to perform their specific function:

  • Anthropic — AI processing (event parsing, chat, briefing generation)
  • Postmark — email delivery and inbound email parsing
  • Twilio — SMS and RCS message delivery
  • Stripe — web payment processing and subscription management
  • Apple — iOS app payment processing
  • Google — Google Calendar OAuth (if you connect your calendar); Maps API for departure time calculations
  • Open-Meteo — weather data (zip code only, no personal data shared)
  • Cloudflare — network traffic, security, and tunnel routing
  • Backblaze — encrypted offsite database backup
  • GitHub / KineticFam — application code version control (no user data)

We do not sell your data to any of these providers, and none of them are permitted to use your information for their own marketing or advertising purposes.

SMS and RCS Text Messaging

Kinetic Family offers an optional text messaging service that delivers your family's daily schedule briefing and event alerts. Messages may be delivered via SMS (Short Message Service) or RCS (Rich Communication Services), depending on your device and carrier. The sender name displayed in your messaging app is Kinetic Family Assistant, sending from toll-free number +1 (844) 991-3182. This section explains how we handle your phone number and all text message communications.

What we collect. When you opt in to text updates, we collect your mobile phone number. We also record the date and time you gave consent, and the version of the consent language you agreed to. We do not collect carrier information or device identifiers.

How it is used. Your phone number is used solely to send you schedule-related messages from Kinetic Family, including:

  • Daily morning briefings with your family’s events, times, and locations (1 per day)
  • Departure alerts when it’s time to leave for a scheduled event (up to 2 per event, on days with events only)
  • Event updates or changes you’ve made to your schedule
  • Occasional service notifications related to your Kinetic account (e.g., trial expiration reminders)

Message frequency: up to 5 messages per day. Most days you will receive 1 morning briefing. On days with events requiring travel, you may also receive up to 2 departure alert messages per event. We do not send unsolicited advertising or third-party promotional messages.

RCS and SMS delivery. Kinetic Family uses RCS Business Messaging (Rich Communication Services) where your device and carrier support it. If your device or carrier does not support RCS, messages automatically fall back to SMS or MMS at no change to you. The same opt-in, opt-out, and content policies apply regardless of delivery method (RCS or SMS).

How to opt in. You may opt in by: (1) checking the SMS/RCS consent checkbox on the Kinetic signup form at kineticfam.com and verifying your number with a one-time code; (2) entering your mobile number in your Kinetic account settings and completing verification; or (3) texting Kinetic’s number at +1 (844) 991-3182 and completing the guided setup. No messages are sent until verification is complete. Full program details: app.kineticfam.com/text-updates.

Consent is voluntary. Text messaging is optional — you can use Kinetic Family fully without enrolling. Checking the consent box is not required to create an account or access any feature. Consent is logged with a timestamp and version identifier and retained as part of your account record.

Consent is not shared with third parties. Your consent to receive messages from Kinetic Family is not transferred, sold, or shared with any other company or program. Consent applies solely to messages sent by Kinetic Family (operated by Kinetic Harmony LLC) from the Kinetic Family Assistant sender.

How to opt out. You can stop receiving all messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any Kinetic text message — you will be unsubscribed immediately and receive one final confirmation. No further messages will be sent from any channel (RCS or SMS).
  • Turning off text messages in your Kinetic account settings
  • Emailing us at admin@kineticfam.com — we will remove your number within 24 hours

You may re-enroll at any time by re-entering your number in your account settings or by replying START to any previous Kinetic message.

Help. Reply HELP to any Kinetic message for assistance, or email admin@kineticfam.com. Full program information is at app.kineticfam.com/text-updates.

Your data is not sold or shared. We never sell, rent, share, or disclose your phone number to any third party for marketing or advertising. Your number is never used to build audience profiles or shared with data brokers.

Message and data rates. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan. Kinetic does not charge separately for text messages. We are not responsible for carrier charges. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

For full program details, message examples, and opt-in/opt-out instructions, visit app.kineticfam.com/text-updates.

Billing & Payment

Kinetic offers a 14-day free trial, after which continued access requires a paid subscription.

What we collect for billing. If you subscribe via the web, your payment is processed by Stripe. If you subscribe through the iOS app, payment is handled by Apple. In both cases, Kinetic Harmony LLC does not store your credit card number, expiration date, or CVV. We only store your subscription status, plan type, and billing period so we know whether your account is active.

What Stripe stores. Stripe stores your payment method securely under their privacy policy and PCI-DSS compliance program. Kinetic receives only a token (customer ID and subscription ID) that lets us verify your subscription status.

Subscription data. We store: whether your subscription is active, your plan tier (monthly/annual), and your trial start/end dates. We do not share this with third parties.

Cancellation. You can cancel at any time from your account settings or the Kinetic dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

Kinetic is a parent-controlled service. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All accounts are created and managed by parents or legal guardians, who control everything about how their family's information is used.

Because Kinetic is built for families, some information you add to your account naturally relates to your children — their names, their school, their sports schedules, and their upcoming events. Here's exactly how we handle that:

  • All children's data flows through you. A parent or guardian creates the account and adds any information about children. Children never sign up directly, and we never collect information from a child without a parent's knowledge and consent.
  • We collect only what's necessary. We store children's names and schedule information only to the extent needed to personalize and deliver your family's morning briefing. Nothing more.
  • No behavioral advertising — ever. We do not use any information about your children (or anyone in your family) for advertising purposes, and we never will.
  • No sharing with third parties. Information about your children is never sold, rented, or disclosed to any third party for any commercial purpose.
  • Emails are never permanently stored. When you forward a school PDF or send us schedule information, the original email is automatically deleted after we extract the relevant dates and events. We keep the extracted events — not your emails.
  • You can review or delete your children's data at any time. Log in to your dashboard at app.kineticfam.com to view, edit, or remove any event or family member. To delete your account and all associated data entirely, visit Account → Delete Account, or email us at admin@kineticfam.com.
  • We will respond to parental data requests within 30 days. If you want to review what information we hold about your child, request corrections, or ask us to stop collecting it, contact us at admin@kineticfam.com.

If we ever discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information directly from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately. Please contact us at admin@kineticfam.com if you have any concerns.

How We Protect Your Data

  • All data is stored securely and access is strictly limited to the Kinetic system — no third parties
  • All connections to Kinetic are encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
  • We regularly review our security practices to make sure your information stays safe
  • Authorized sender verification means strangers can't add events to your calendar, even if they guess your Kinetic email address
  • Emails you send to your Kinetic address are automatically deleted after processing — we extract the dates and events, then the original email is permanently removed. Your personal emails are never stored.

You're Always in Control

  • View your data anytime at app.kineticfam.com
  • Edit or delete any event directly from your dashboard
  • Add or remove family members anytime from your account settings
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing admin@kineticfam.com — we'll send it within 30 days

  • Unsubscribe from any email using the link in the footer, or by emailing us directly

Data Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently remove all associated data — including your family's name, email addresses, schedule events, and any other information we hold — within 30 days of your request.

Inbound emails sent to your Kinetic address are deleted immediately after processing. We never archive or retain the original content of emails you send us.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy — particularly anything affecting how children's information is handled — we'll notify you by email before those changes take effect. The date at the top of this page always reflects when it was last updated.
