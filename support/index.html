❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add events to my schedule?

You can add events by typing in the chat assistant ("Add soccer Tuesday at 5pm"), by uploading a photo of a flyer or schedule in the app, or by forwarding any confirmation email to your family's Kinetic address.

My briefing email isn't arriving. What should I check?

Check your spam folder first — look for an email from briefing@kineticfam.com. If it's there, mark it as not spam. If it's not there at all, email us and we'll investigate.

How do I connect my Google Calendar?

Open the Kinetic app → Connect tab → Google Calendar → tap to connect. You'll be prompted to sign in with Google. We request read-only access — we never modify your calendar.

How do I add a caregiver?

Open the app → Family tab → Caregivers → tap "Share access link." This generates a read-only link you can share with a babysitter, grandparent, or anyone who needs to see your family's schedule.

How do I cancel my subscription?

For subscriptions purchased through the App Store, go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Kinetic Family → Cancel. For subscriptions purchased on the web, email us at support@kineticfam.com and we'll cancel it right away.

How do I delete my account?

Open the Kinetic app → tap the gear icon on the Family tab → scroll to the bottom → "Delete Account." This permanently removes your account and all data. If you're having trouble, email us and we'll delete it manually within 24 hours.

What data does Kinetic collect?

We collect your email address, family name, zip code, and the events you add to your schedule. If you connect Google Calendar, we access your calendar read-only. Location is used for weather and departure alerts and is never stored permanently. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

📱 App Issues

If the app is crashing or behaving unexpectedly, try these steps:

1. Close and reopen the app
2. Check for a TestFlight update
3. Delete and reinstall the app (your account data is stored on our servers — nothing is lost)
4. If none of those work, email us with a description of what happened
