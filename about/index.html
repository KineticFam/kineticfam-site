Born from Family Chaos

Kinetic was built by a parent who got tired of being the only one who knew what was happening this week.

The Problem We Solved First for Ourselves

Every family has that one person who holds the schedule. Not because they volunteered — because someone had to. They're the one who knows about the field trip on Thursday, the dentist appointment that got moved, the birthday party nobody else remembered to put in the calendar. They carry it all, quietly, in their head.

That's not a personality trait. It's a systems problem. And systems problems have solutions.

Kinetic started as a personal project — a way to automatically compile the family schedule and deliver it every morning before the chaos started. Forward the school newsletter. Snap a photo of the soccer schedule on the fridge. Attach the camp confirmation PDF. Kinetic reads it all, pulls out the relevant dates, and delivers a clean morning briefing at 6:30 AM.

No app to remember to open. No shared calendar that one person updates and the other ignores. Just one email every morning with everything your family needs to know that day.

What We Believe

Technology should simplify family life, not add to it. Most family organization tools are really just another thing to manage — another app, another login, another notification stream. We built Kinetic to work the opposite way: it comes to you, not the other way around.

We also believe your family's schedule is private. Kinetic never connects to your personal email or calendar accounts. It only knows what you choose to send it. Your data is used exclusively to generate your daily briefing — it's never sold, shared, or used to train AI models.
