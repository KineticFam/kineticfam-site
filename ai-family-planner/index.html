What is an AI family planner?

An AI family planner is a tool that handles the logistics of family scheduling automatically — reading your emails, parsing PDF schedules, pulling school calendars, and keeping every family member informed without any manual effort on your part.

Kinetic is the first AI family planner built around your inbox, not another app. Instead of asking you to enter events manually or remember to open yet another calendar, Kinetic works in the background. You forward an email, and Kinetic handles the rest. Every morning, your family wakes up to a personalized briefing with everything they need to know about the day.

Think of it as a family chief of staff who processes all the incoming information and delivers a clear, concise daily brief to every parent and family member who needs it.

How Kinetic's AI actually works

Kinetic isn't just a calendar app with "AI" slapped on it. It's an end-to-end AI pipeline that processes messy, real-world family information and produces a clean, structured daily output.

📥

Intake: emails, PDFs, photos

Your family's information arrives in a hundred formats — forwarded emails, attached PDFs, photos of paper fliers. Kinetic accepts all of them at your dedicated family email address.

🤖

AI parsing: extracting what matters

Claude (Anthropic's AI) reads the content and extracts structured event data: title, date, time, location, who it's for. It handles ambiguity, informal language, and complex multi-event documents.

🗄️

Context: merging with your family's calendar

Parsed events are merged with your existing calendar, school district holidays, iCal subscriptions, and sports schedules to build a complete, unified picture of what's happening for your family.

☀️

Generation: the daily briefing

Every morning at 6:28 AM, Kinetic generates a personalized briefing for each family member — pulling today's events, weather, news, and school reminders into a readable, beautiful email delivered by 6:30 AM.
