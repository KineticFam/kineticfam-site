What an AI Family Planner Actually Means

Most apps called "AI" use artificial intelligence to answer questions. Kinetic uses it differently: AI reads your emails, photos, and documents so you don't have to. You forward a school newsletter — AI extracts every date. You attach a sports schedule PDF — AI pulls out every game. You snap a photo of the paper calendar on your fridge — AI reads it.

The output is always the same: one clean email every morning with everything your family needs to know that day. The AI is in the input, not the output. Which means the briefing is always readable, always useful, and never feels like you're talking to a robot.
